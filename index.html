Description

Technical writing is a type of writing that effectively and efficiently conveys information about a subject. This type of writing is used to create manuals, professional correspondence, and guides for different scenarios and levels of understanding. This portfolio will look at a few of the most crucial elements used in technical writing; whether they are technical writing strategies, types of documents, or aesthetic.
