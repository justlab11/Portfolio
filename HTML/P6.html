Hook for a Future Cover Letter

Prompt

This portfolio prompt is the start of a cover letter that I could use in the future. I considered where I would like to work and what I would like to do.

Portfolio Piece

The field of electrical engineering relies heavily on math and coding, which are two hobbies of mine. I enjoy figuring out how the things we use in our daily lives are designed and all of the small, but important, elements that go into their design.

My ideal career would consist of using hardware and software to solve complex tasks. Working with computers and using creativity to solve problems is something that I hope to do in the future.

My goals for my next job are to start my electrical engineering career and to apply the knowledge that I have been learning for the past four years. Since I have never had a job before, I am excited to enter into the field of electrical engineering and learn new things.

To improve the likelihood of getting a job, my next step is to get an internship or do undergraduate research. I am currently discussing a potential research opportunity with Professor Brockmeier; he works with biomedical image classification and machine learning algorithms. After getting some experience in the field, the next step is to decide where in the world I would like to work. This step is the key to determining the job positions that will be available to me. After picking the location, I will have to start looking and applying for jobs. Once I get a job, I will have achieved all that I set out to accomplish.

I am a recent graduate of the University of Delaware, where I studied electrical engineering. I am passionate about this position because machine learning is something that I have taken the time to learn throughout college. I have worked with graduate students and professors to improve my machine learning abilities. I hope to apply what I have learned to real-world projects.
